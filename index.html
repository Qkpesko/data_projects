About Me

Hi, I'm Pelumi Okunola — a data & business analyst with 6+ years of experience working in fintech, payments, and operations. I currently lead the Fiat Team at Quidax, where I oversee ₦10B+ in weekly transaction volumes across three core fiat products.

I started my career in banking, transitioned into crypto during the Nigerian ban, and helped Quidax build its peer-to-peer fiat infrastructure from scratch. I’ve since grown the team to 7 people and built operational systems that are both resilient and scalable.

As someone obsessed with clarity, my tools of choice are SQL, Python, Excel, and Looker Studio. I’ve used them to identify fraud patterns, reduce drop-offs in user funnels, build dashboards executives actually use, and lead projects like our expansion into Ghana.

I’m currently studying for an MSc in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science at the University of Hull to deepen my statistical and machine learning skills. My next step? A career that blends data, operations, and strategy — ideally in a place that moves fast and thinks smart.

Outside work, I’m a forex trader, reader, and someone committed to spiritual and personal growth. I’m focused on maintaining consistency across all areas of life, and learning something new every week — just to stay curious and whole.
