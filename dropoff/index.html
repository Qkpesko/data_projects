Customer Volume Drop-Off & Recovery

This project analyzes user behavior across weekly deposit data to identify volume drop-offs, track recovery patterns, and quantify risk signals.

It includes cohort analysis, feature engineering for drop-off detection, and strategic insight generation for fintech growth and ops teams.
